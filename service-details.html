Arbitration

Our arbitration services provide a legally binding resolution to disputes through a structured, impartial process. Our experienced arbitrators ensure fair and enforceable decisions that comply with international and domestic laws.

Mediation

Through professional mediation services, we help disputing parties find common ground and reach mutually beneficial solutions. Our mediators facilitate productive discussions that lead to voluntary and sustainable agreements.

Online Dispute Resolution (ODR)

Leveraging technology, we offer ODR services that enable parties to resolve conflicts remotely. This process is ideal for commercial disputes, consumer complaints, and cross-border transactions.

How It Works?

1. Case Assessment – Submit your dispute details for an initial evaluation. Our experts will review the case and determine the most suitable dispute resolution approach.
2. Selection of Arbitrator/Mediator – We assign a qualified expert with industry-specific knowledge to your case, ensuring fairness and expertise in the process.
3. ADR Process Initiation – Depending on the chosen method (arbitration or mediation), sessions begin with structured discussions, evidence presentation, and negotiations facilitated by our specialists.
4. Settlement or Award – In mediation, a mutually agreed-upon resolution is documented for compliance. In arbitration, the arbitrator delivers a final, legally binding award.
5. Closure & Compliance – We assist parties in ensuring compliance with the settlement or arbitration award, helping implement enforceable resolutions.
6. Post-Resolution Support – If needed, we provide follow-up assistance, guidance on compliance, and resources to ensure long-term conflict resolution.

Arbitration is a private dispute resolution process where an impartial arbitrator makes a binding decision based on presented evidence and arguments.

Mediation is a voluntary process where a neutral mediator helps parties negotiate a mutually acceptable agreement without imposing a decision.
