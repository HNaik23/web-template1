Common Misconceptions About Mediation and Arbitration

Mediation and arbitration are two widely used methods of Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR), offering parties a more efficient and cost-effective way to resolve disputes outside of traditional court proceedings. However, there are many misconceptions about these processes that often lead to confusion or hesitation in their adoption. This article aims to debunk some of the most common myths surrounding mediation and arbitration.
